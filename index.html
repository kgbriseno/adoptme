Looking to Adopt a Pet?

Around 1.5 million rescue animals are euthanized annually (670,000 dogs and 860,000 cats). By choosing to adopt, not only are you saving a life, but you are also contributing to a healthier lifestyle for yourself. Not only do furry friends provide unconditional love, but the also provide us with psychological, emotional, and physical benefits. Still not sold?
